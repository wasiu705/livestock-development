HR / Administrative Department

Driving organizational excellence through strategic human resource management, staff welfare, and seamless administrative operations.

Mrs. Ojezua

Director, Administration & Human Resources

Leads the Ministry's human capital strategy, ensuring operational excellence across all administrative functions. With extensive experience in public sector HR management, she oversees workforce planning, staff welfare, policy implementation, and seamless day-to-day operations to keep the Ministry optimally manned and primed for high performance.

Contact Department

Department Mandate

Responsible for managing the MDA’s human resources along the full value chain of HR management, in areas of manpower planning, succession planning, talent management, and career management of MDA staff to ensure the MDA is optimally manned, with properly managed, aligned and high performing staff, primed to deliver the MDA’s mandate.

Key Responsibilities

Strategic functions driving workforce optimization and administrative excellence.

Human Resources Management

  • Manpower planning, establishment control & succession planning
  • Staff recruitment, selection, placement & onboarding
  • Learning & development, training coordination & competency assessment
  • Performance appraisal, career management & promotion processing
  • Employee relations counseling, disciplinary handling & statutory compliance
  • Compensation administration, pension management & benefits processing
  • Workplace health, safety & staff welfare oversight

General Administration

  • Office equipment maintenance, requisition & supply chain coordination
  • Establishment proposal preparation & budget estimate liaison with Finance
  • Records management, confidential filing system & ministry database maintenance
  • Executive scheduling, appointment organization & calendar management
  • Management information reports, statistical data & presentations
  • Inter-departmental coordination & administrative support services
  • Workspace infrastructure & environment optimization per EDSG policy

Administration Officer - Supply & Logistics

  • Generate routine reports and manage procurement requisition processes
  • Balance stakeholder interests (staff, suppliers, local/state government & host communities)
  • Receive, verify & log incoming consignments and goods
  • Carry out comprehensive office & departmental inventory management
  • Safe keeping, custody control & distribution of ministry assets
  • Coordinate disposal of boarded vehicles, equipment & obsolete goods
