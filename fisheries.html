Fisheries Department

Promoting sustainable aquatic resource management, aquaculture development, and responsible fisheries practices for food security and economic growth.

Mr.Akhabue Aaron Osaigbovo

Director, Fisheries Department

Leads the strategic development and sustainable management of Edo State's fisheries and aquatic resources. With a strong background in aquaculture science and regulatory governance, he drives policy implementation, capacity building for fish farmers, and enforcement of fisheries laws to ensure food security, economic empowerment, and ecological conservation.

Contact Department

Department Mandate

  • To promote sustainable development, management, and utilization of fisheries and aquatic resources
  • To formulate policy and regulations that facilitate responsible fisheries management and operation
  • To enhance food security through the provision of animal protein (FISH)
  • To ensure equitable access to fisheries resources
  • To support economic growth while ensuring the conservation of aquatic ecosystems

Divisions: Roles & Responsibilities

Specialized units driving sustainable aquaculture, responsible capture fisheries, and regulatory compliance.

Aquaculture

  • Develop aquaculture policies, strategies and standards
  • Promote sustainable aquaculture practices and biosecurity measures
  • Oversee fish hatcheries, breed programs and seed production
  • Support capacity buildings and trainings for aquaculture stakeholders
  • Provide advisory services and technical support to fish farmers

Capture Fisheries Governance

  • Support data collection, research and stock assessment activities
  • Promote responsible fishing practices and community-based fisheries management
  • Coordinate surveillance and compliance programs against illegal and unregulated fishing
  • Facilitate the construction and maintenance of fishing infrastructure such as landing jetties and fish markets
  • Provide technical guidance on fishing gear, vessel operation and post-harvest handling

Licensing & Regulatory Services

  • Registration and renewal of fish trade licenses and permits
  • Enforcement of fisheries laws and regulation
  • Conduct inspections and compliance audit of fisheries operations
  • Investigate and address fisheries related offenses and violations
  • Provide advisory services on policy reforms, legal frameworks and regulatory improvement
