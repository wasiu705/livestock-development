About The Ministry

Driving sustainable growth, innovation, and policy excellence in Edo State's livestock and fisheries sector.

Our Story & Mandate

The Ministry of Livestock Development was established to transform Edo State's agricultural landscape by promoting modern, sustainable, and technology-driven livestock practices. We work closely with farmers, investors, and development partners to ensure food security, job creation, and rural economic empowerment.

Through strategic policy implementation, capacity building, and infrastructure development, we aim to position Edo State as a national leader in livestock production, animal health management, and fisheries development.

Our Vision

To be the leading state in Nigeria for sustainable, technology-driven livestock production and management, ensuring food security and wealth creation for all stakeholders.

Our Mission

To formulate and implement policies that promote modern livestock practices, enhance animal health services, and create an enabling environment for private sector investment.

Our Mandate

Regulate, develop, and promote livestock resources through veterinary services, quality assurance, research collaboration, and farmer capacity development initiatives.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Transparent, accountable, and ethical in all operations.

Innovation

Embracing modern technology and best practices.

Inclusivity

Empowering youth, women, and rural farmers.

Sustainability

Environmentally conscious and future-focused.
