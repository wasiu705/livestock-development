About The Ministry

Established to drive sustainable growth in Edo State's livestock industry through strategic policy implementation, infrastructure development, and capacity building.

Our Vision

A modern, inclusive, and sustainable livestock and fisheries sector driving food security, public health, and economic prosperity for all in Edo State.

Our Mission

Advance sustainable livestock and fisheries development in Edo State through evidence-based policies, robust disease control, and stringent food safety regulation, while fostering agribusiness empowerment to strengthen economic resilience and shared prosperity.

Our Mandate

  • Strategic leadership and policy direction for sustainable growth of the livestock and fisheries sectors, advancing food and nutrition security and inclusive economic development.
  • Transformation of production systems and value chains into modern, efficient, and resilient models, positioning Edo State as a leading livestock hub in Nigeria.
  • Regulation of animal health, welfare, biosecurity, sanitary and phytosanitary standards, and food safety, to protect consumers and enable safe domestic and international trade.
  • Strategic oversight of livestock identification, traceability, and orderly animal movement, to support disease control and prevent conflicts linked to uncontrolled mobility.
  • Promotion of agribusiness development, private sector participation, market access, and value addition, with emphasis on job creation and youth and women empowerment.
