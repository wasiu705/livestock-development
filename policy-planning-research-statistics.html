Policy, Planning, Research & Statistics

Driving evidence-based policy, strategic planning, and robust data analytics to guide sustainable livestock sector growth in Edo State.

Mrs. Osawemwenze Deborah

Director, Policy, Planning, Research & Statistics

Leads the Ministry's strategic planning, research, and data analytics framework. She oversees policy formulation, macro-economic analysis, budget development, and ICT integration to ensure seamless, evidence-based decision-making across all livestock and fisheries programs.

Contact Department

Department Mandate

  • Facilitate research on good livestock practices for the state, establish a data bank on livestock, and ensure systematic data collection, processing, and analysis in line with national standards.
  • Provide seamless integrated services through the use of ICT for greater efficiency and effectiveness in achieving all departmental objectives.

Departmental Roles & Responsibilities

  • Develop livestock ranking system and agricultural directory
  • Update agro-statistics and maintain the state data bank
  • Create and manage ministry dashboards and technical support units
  • Develop strategic plans and standards for livestock institutions in the state
  • Create and manage the official ministry website and digital platforms
  • Provide data-driven policy advice to ministry leadership
  • Coordinate, collate, and render periodic reports on Ministry's activities to farmers and stakeholders

Key Responsibilities by Unit

Core operational divisions driving policy, research, project monitoring, and strategic budgeting.

Policy Formulation

  • Develop, implement, and monitor the ministry's strategic plan in collaboration with key personnel
  • Collect, analyse, and compile data on social, economic, and sectoral issues
  • Coordinate annual strategic planning, performance goals, and budget preparation processes
  • Manage KPI scorecards and facilitate monthly/quarterly performance review meetings
  • Develop, review, and monitor policies affecting the ministry and livestock sector
  • Develop implementation plans, evaluate policy impacts, and liaise with relevant institutions on policy matters

Research & Business Intelligence

  • Produce accurate, timely reports and analytics for evidence-based decision making
  • Collate research data to identify emerging trends and inform policy formulation
  • Provide reliable agricultural statistics and design questionnaires for data collection
  • Compile and publish Annual/Quarterly Economic and Business reports
  • Conduct adaptive research to improve yields, reduce losses, and enhance food quality
  • Participate in national livestock research conferences and liaise with institutes (IITA, NIFOR, etc.) to transfer new technologies to ministry departments

Project Management, Monitoring & Evaluation

  • Develop systematic monitoring frameworks to improve qualitative and quantitative evidence
  • Provide technical assistance to implementing agencies on monitoring protocols
  • Collaborate with departments on project execution and progress reporting
  • Assist in revising project logframes, performance indicators, and measurement tools
  • Assess program effects, impacts, and sustainability
  • Report monthly, quarterly, and annually on project activities and prepare management decision reports
  • Develop impact indicators and measure/report on project performance

Planning, Statistics & Budget Development

  • Conduct focused analytical studies and state macro-economic analysis
  • Analyze economic data to provide macro-fiscal policy advice and forecasts
  • Liaise with NBS, NIPSS, universities, and other MDAs for data sharing
  • Contribute to state economic policy development using gathered intelligence
  • Develop livestock sector plans, budgeting processes, and cost projections
  • Consolidate budget data, analyze variances, and prepare amendment reports
  • Participate in annual budget performance appraisals and coordinate medium-term economic framework preparation
